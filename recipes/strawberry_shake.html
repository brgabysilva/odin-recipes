Strawberry Shake

I love this recipe, easy and healthy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine milk, honey, vanilla and frozen strawberries
  2. Blen until smooth
  3. Pour into galsses and serve

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving186 calories; protein 8.4g; carbohydrates 27.1g; fat 4.9g; cholesterol 19.5mg; sodium 102.1mg.
